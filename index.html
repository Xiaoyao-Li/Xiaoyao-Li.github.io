Puhao Li(李浦豪)

I am a senior undergraduate student in Dept. of Automatoin, Tsinghua University. Before that, I was educated in Huizhou, Guangdong. I am also a research intern at Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence(BIGAI), supervised by Dr. Tengyu Liu and Dr. Siyuan Huang. I obtained my pre-admission of Ph.D. student from Tsinghua University in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Song-Chun Zhu.

My research interests lie in computer vision, generative model and robotic grasping. I currently study in the problem of generalizable robotic grasping policy from vision and generation.

Research
GenDexGrasp: Generalizable Dexterous Grasping
Puhao Li*, Tengyu Liu*, Yuyang Li, Yiran Geng, Yixin Zhu, Yaodong Yang, Siyuan Huang
ICRA 2023, Under Review
[arXiv]   [PDF]   [Project]   [Code]
We introduce GenDexGrasp, a versatile dexterous grasping method that can generalize to out-of-domain robotic hands. In addition, we contribute MultiDex, a large-scale synthetic dexterous grasping dataset.
DexGraspNet: A Large-Scale Robotic Dexterous Grasp Dataset for General Objects Based on Simulation
Ruicheng Wang*, Jialiang Zhang*, Jiayi Chen, Yinzhen Xu, Puhao Li, Tengyu Liu, He Wang
ICRA 2023, Under Review
[arXiv]
We present a large-scale dexterous grasping dataset DexGraspNet, which based on simulation. This dataset features more physical stability and higher diversity than previous datasets.
